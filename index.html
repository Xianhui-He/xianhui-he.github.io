Xianhui He

I am a master student in psychology working with Dr.Ying Cai at Zhejiang University (Hangzhou, China).

My long-term interest is to understand how human brain transforms visual representation from perception to working memory (WM) to long-term memory. I mainly used iEEG/EEG, tES and computational models in my research.

I graduated with a BSci degree in psychology at Zhejiang University. You can find my CV here.

In my spare time, I enjoy watching movies (my favorite director is Ryusuke Hamaguchi) and playing volleballs.
